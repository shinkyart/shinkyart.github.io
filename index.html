Ebenezer Attachie

Ebenezer Attachie is a Ghanaian painter based in Accra, where the vibrant city life and rich cultural heritage serve as endless inspiration for his art. Working with mediums like charcoal, graphite, and acrylics, Attachie creates thought-provoking pieces that reflect the world around him. His art is a testament to the beauty and complexity of everyday life in Ghana, inviting viewers to experience the sights, sounds, and emotions that shape his world.
